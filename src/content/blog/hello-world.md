We're excited to launch the Buxo blog! This is where we'll share product updates, deep dives into our scheduling engine, and thoughts on how intent-based scheduling is changing the way people book meetings.

## What is Buxo?

Buxo is a next-generation scheduling platform that goes beyond simple calendar availability. We use intent-based scheduling to suggest the *best* times to meet — not just the open ones.

## What to expect

We'll be writing about:

- **Product updates** — new features, improvements, and what's coming next
- **Engineering deep dives** — how we built our slot scoring algorithm, our real-time availability engine, and more
- **Scheduling insights** — tips and best practices for making the most of your calendar

Stay tuned for more posts. In the meantime, check out [buxo.ai](https://buxo.ai) to learn more about what we're building.
